Listening to Music

One of my favorite activities during the day is listening to music. I tend to use every opportunity I can to do so. My favorite genre is chillhop, with a lot of lo-fi. I discovered the genre in 2017, and I've been listening to it since then, adding tracks I discover to a playlist that's now over 13 hours long. Koto City by Mama Aiuto is a personal favorite of mine that I found in 2020. Polaroids of Ipanema by Jazzinuf is also a favorite of mine. Outside of the usual, I tend to get nostalgic for popular songs from the 2000's and early 2010's, so I listen to those a lot as well.
